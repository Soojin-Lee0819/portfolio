What's Good Today?

Hi there! I am a creative artist from South Korea, who grew up China, and currently studying at New York University Abu Dhabi. In this website, I am sharing four multimedia projects and websites I made for a class called Communication Lab. I hope my projects share some positive messages and creative inspirations. Hope you enjoy!

Instead, Distance Socialize

We challenged to make a shiort film within 30 minutes. It shares a message to stay safe, connected, and socialize safely.

Life is Unpredictable

This is an interactive web-comic project. It shares a message that you can't connect the dots looking forward; you can only connect them looking backwards.

Hear Our Spirits

This is a sound project with some visual elements. It invites people to experience life on NYUAD during COVID-19 through audio.

How To Self Care

This is a stop-motion video project. It encourages self-care practices and shares fun self-care ideas.
